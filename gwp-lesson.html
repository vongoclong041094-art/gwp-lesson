üìã 2.5 Regulations Around GWP

Key Regulations:

  • Kigali Amendment (2016): Global plan to phase down HFCs
  • U.S. EPA SNAP Program: Regulates acceptable refrigerants based on both ODP & GWP
  • Technician responsibility: Choose refrigerants that meet environmental standards and handle high-GWP gases carefully (no venting)
